Welcome

Welcome to my homepage. I am Yaoqi Chen (陈耀祺), a joint Ph.D. student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), where I am also a research intern in the system research group. I am fortunate to be co-advised by Prof. Cheng Li at USTC, alongside Dr. Fan Yang, Dr. Qianxi Zhang, and Dr. Baotong Lu at MSRA. My primary research interests lie in vector search, agentic AI systems (e.g., long-term memory), and machine learning systems (e.g., efficient long-context models).

During my undergraduate studies at USTC, I achieved the highest GPA in the School of Computer Science. I also participated in many competitions, including the 10th Asia Supercomputer Community Student Supercomputer Challenge (ASC22-23). Under the guidance of Prof. Hong An, I optimized the training speed of deep molecular dynamics models (i.e., DeePMD-kit) on supercomputers, helping our team secure the second place in the final competition. In recognition of these achievements, I was honored with the National Scholarship and the Guo Moruo Scholarship, the highest undergraduate distinction at USTC.

As a Ph.D. student, I am driven by the pursuit of knowledge and a desire to build the next generation of AI infrastructure. I am eager to make meaningful contributions to the advancement of machine learning and agentic systems, continually seeking ways to enhance their efficiency and scalability.
